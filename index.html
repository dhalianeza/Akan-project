Akan Names

A web application that takes a user's birthday and calculates the day of the week
they were born and then depending on their gender outputs
their Akan Name.Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture.
Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name
as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.

Here are the Ghanian's names

    Male

  • Sunday: - Kwasi
  • Monday: - Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: - Kwabena
  • Wednesday: - Kwaku
  • Thursday: - Yaw
  • Friday: - Kofi
  • Saturday: - Kwame

    Female
